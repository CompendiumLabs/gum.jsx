# Punk Rock

This one is very simple, but it is a nice reminder that layout elements can be graphic design tools too. The three [TextFrame](/docs/TextBox) boxes are arranged with an `HStack`, given different corner roundings, and then wrapped in a larger rotated frame to get the sticker-like composition.

Most of the energy comes from the framing rather than the text itself. The bright blocks, asymmetric rounding, and overall rotation are enough to push the piece toward a poster or logo treatment with very little code.
